FOR DANCERS, MUSICIANS, SINGERS, TEACHER, POETS, PERFORMERS

Practice, Loop - Loop, Practice!

The Practice Looper app puts an end to the constant rewinding to loop only parts of your favourite songs from different audio sources - including songs from your Spotify Premium account.

Audio-Files

Play audio files of up to 60 minutes playback length.

Other streaming services

More streaming providers will follow soon - stay up to date and follow our social media channels!

Spotify

Use your Spotify Premium account to add songs from the entire Spotify library.

Slider

Use the slider to set the start and end marker of your desired loop. Short loops of at least five seconds are possible.

Picker

You can also use the picker to adjust your loop to the second - the tutorial in the app shows you how.

Premium Feature

These feature is available in the Premium Version. You can find more about this here.

Create & Save Loops

You can create and save multiple loops for each song. Give your loops names, such as "Intro" or "Refrain".

Load & Delete Loops

Saved loops can be viewed by pressing the button in the line next to the song. Here you can delete or load and play your loops by selecting them.
